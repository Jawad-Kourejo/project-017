EXECUTIVE LEADERSHIP

CHIEF OPERATING OFFICER – IMDAAD

MAHMOOD RASHEED

Mahmood Rasheed is the Chief Operating Officer at Imdaad and was the Executive Director of Imdaad’s Operations. Rasheed has a comprehensive experience on EHS procedures that he attained while working at Dubai World since 1996, in the Regulatory Authority, handling diverse environmental issues of the Ports, Free Zone and Nakheel Projects.

CHIEF FINANCIAL OFFICER - IMDAAD

FATMA MOHAMMAD AHLI

Fatma Mohammad Ahli serves as Chief Financial Officer at the Imdaad Group, where she has consistently demonstrated a collaborative and transformative leadership style. She has an impressive career spanning over two decades in financial management within the government and semi-government sectors.

Throughout her professional journey, Fatma built a strong foundation of knowledge through diverse roles and internships at Dubai Municipality, Emirates Airlines, DNATA, RTA, Axiom, and PWC. Fatma held key management positions in organizations that made substantial contributions to the development of modern Dubai. Prior to her current role, Fatma’s journey in finance has been marked by significant roles, including Executive Director of Finance at TECOM Investments, where she played a pivotal role in strategic financial planning and operational enhancements. Her expertise lies in developing comprehensive financial strategies, enhancing operational efficiencies, and steering her teams toward achieving exemplary financial outcomes.

More recently, at Emirates Post Group, in the capacity of Director of Business Units and Financial Operations, Fatma led a team focused on optimizing financial operations and business unit performance. She oversaw the financial planning, budgeting, and reporting processes. Her role involved close collaboration with other business unit heads to ensure goal alignment with the broader corporate strategic objectives.

Fatma holds a bachelor’s degree in accounting from Dubai Women’s College and a Diploma in European Foundation Quality Management (EFQM). She is a certified Project Management Professional (PMP) and certified in Business Continuity Management (BCM). Fatma completed a Women Leadership Exchange Program from Dubai Women Establishment (Sweden & UAE).

Fatma possesses a deep-seated passion for financial analysis modeling and forecasting, investment analysis, credit management, finance audit, and organizational restructuring.

As a distinguished business leader, Fatma was recognized for her achievements by prestigious awarding bodies, receiving various awards, including TECOM Best People Manager, Best Savings Initiative Award, The First Star of Finance Department, the Yearly Winner of Emirates Post Making A Difference Award, and a Certificate of Appreciation from the Dubai Government Excellence Program.

Fatma is an inspirational leader and an advocate for integrating best practices in the financial domain. Her commitment to excellence and sustainability in financial operations makes her a great asset to the Imdaad Group, continuously driving the organization towards higher profitability and efficiency.

CHIEF SUPPORT OFFICER (CFO)

SAEED AHMED

Saeed Ahmed brings over two decades of Facilities Management expertise to his role as Chief Support Officer at Imdaad Group, where he leads the organization’s support functions, including Strategy & Performance, Mergers & Acquisitions, New Market Entry, Health & Safety, Quality, Innovation & COE, Sustainability, and Supply Chain. Since joining Imdaad in 2021, Saeed has demonstrated exceptional strategic leadership, spearheading initiatives that have enhanced operational excellence and driven the company’s regional expansion and long-term growth. His pivotal role reflects the alignment of the company’s support framework with its ambitious business goals and vision for the future.

Saeed is a proven board-level company director with an outstanding track record in the Facilities Management industry. He has worked with leading FM providers across the UK and the Middle East, managing operations in the UAE, Qatar, KSA, and Oman. Originally a Supply Chain specialist, Saeed successfully completed Executive Development Programs with his previous employers and has held leadership roles across various FM business departments, including operations, business development, change management, commercial, marketing, and business improvement. This diverse experience has established him as a respected Facilities Management expert capable of influencing all aspects of a business.

In his role as Chief Support Officer, Saeed collaborates closely with Imdaad’s Business Units and support functions to ensure a cohesive strategic vision aligned with the Group Business Plan and shareholder aspirations. He continues to guide the development and implementation of Excellence Plans, enabling the organization to achieve its strategic targets. He also monitors the performance of all business units and support functions using Balanced Scorecards and performance management tools.

Saeed leads Imdaad’s initiatives for inorganic growth, including market expansion, the introduction of new service offerings, and mergers and acquisitions (M&A). His strategic foresight and leadership have been instrumental in driving Imdaad’s growth and maintaining its position as a leader in the FM sector.

Extremely commercially aware with a sharp analytical mind, Saeed has extensive experience managing large, complex projects and teams. His meticulous attention to detail and ability to identify and mitigate risks ensure efficient and effective delivery across all his responsibilities. Saeed holds a B.Sc. in Forensic and Analytical Chemistry from the University of Strathclyde in the UK and has spent the last 27 years building an exemplary career in Facilities Management. His previous employers include Balfour Beatty and Carillion in the UK, as well as Emrill, Interserve, and Khidmah in the Middle East.
